Swedish Massage

This is the type of massage that everyone thinks about when they envision a "massage." All physical movements during the massage are applied in the direction of the heart to encourage blood circulation and disposal of bodily waste.

Deep Tissue Massage

This type of massage is used to heal adhesions or "knots" in your muscle fibers and connective tissue. By applying and manipulating a specific muscle or connective tissue area with very strong pressure, the muscle and connective tissue is helped in its repair and restoration to a healthy state.

Couples Massage

This type of massage is performed in the Swedish style with any two persons. There is no better way to relax and enjoy the company of a lover, spouse, or good friend than with a couples massage.

Foot Reflexology

This type of massage only pertains to the feet. In essence, foot reflexology is the practice of stimulating nerves in the foot that lead to relaxation and through this, stress reduction that leads to better overall health.

Hot Stone Therapy

This type of massage consists of placing smooth heated stones at strategic places on the back. The heated stones relieve stiffness and soreness in the back muscles and surrounding joints, and when followed by a massage helps to restore the muscle and connective tissue to full health.

Aromatherapy Massage

This type of treatment uses different essential oils in combination with Swedish or Deep Tissue massage. The essential oils are chosen by your therapist to stimulate the olfactory (smell) system to achieve a desired emotional state of the mind.

Salt Scrub and Massage

This type of treatment consists of being rubbed with sea salt mixed with aromatic oils that hydrate and exfoliate the skin all at once. After the salt scrub a shower is taken followed by a relaxing massage.

Shiatsu Massage

This treatment consists of applying varying pressure with the fingers, elbows, or palms to specific areas on the skin. When these specific areas are massaged, a tremendous feeling of relaxation results. Another fringe benefit of this treatment is increased circulation and lymphatic flow.

Basic Facial

This type of treatment will make your facial skin look healthier and more beautiful. Your face will undergo a steam treatment, cleansing and exfoliation, a hydrating treatment with a facial mask, concluding with a face and neck massage.

Serenity Facial

This treatment is the same as the Basic Facial with the addition of skin care for your hands. Your hands will be treated with cleansing, exfoliation, hydrating lotions, and creams with heated gloves on both hands ending with a hand massage.

Acne Treatment

This treatment utilizes lotions with alpha-hydroxy acids, urea peroxide, and salicylic acid for cleansing skin of whiteheads and blackheads. This treatment effectively inhibits excessive skin hormones with special organic acids, zinc, and vitamins.

Ultrasonic Facial

This treatment is performed by applying sound waves to generate pressure on deep skin tissue. Deep cleansing, exfoliation, vapor spray, blackhead removal, and eyebrow shaping are also done during the procedure. The treatment ends with a face and neck massage.
