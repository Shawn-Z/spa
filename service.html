FAQS

What should I wear to my massage appointment?

Wear comfortable clothing. Depending on the treatment you choose, you may or may not need to completely disrobe. Your massage therapist will give you privacy to undress, and you will be covered with sheets or towels during your treatment.

What are your payment policies?

We accept payment by cash or credit card (Visa or MasterCard). Local regulations require that patrons pay at the time of service. If you would like to tip your massage therapist, you are welcome to do so at the end of your treatment session.

Are your massage oils hypoallergenic?

All of the materials we use are designed to minimize allergic reactions.  If you are highly sensitive, we would be happy to test these items on a small patch of skin first, to ensure your comfort.

What do I do during a massage therapy treatment?

Make yourself comfortable. This is a time for you to relax. If your therapist requires you to be in a specific position, she will let you know.
