Policy

We accept payment by cash or credit card (Visa or MasterCard). Local regulations require that patrons pay at the time of service. If you would like to tip your massage therapist, you are welcome to do so at the end of your treatment session.

If you have scheduled an appointment and need to cancel or reschedule, please give us at least 4 hours notice. This will allow us to serve other customers during that time.

We do not have daycare facilities. Please make other arrangements for your children so that you can fully enjoy your relaxing time with us.

Please arrive 5-10 minutes ahead of your scheduled appointment time.

We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for inappropriate conduct or behavior.

No refunds will be issued after the treatment has started.  However, we are more than happy to change your masseuse during the first 15 minutes of your massage if you are not completely satisfied.
